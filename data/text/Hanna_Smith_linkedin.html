Bio: Hanna Smith is a passionate baker and the proud owner of Hannas French Pastries. She specializes in creating delicious and authentic French pastries, which she sells at the Palo Alto Farmers Market every Sunday. With the help of her two teenage daughters, Hanna ensures that each pastry is made with love and attention to detail. Her dedication to her craft and commitment to providing high-quality pastries have earned her a loyal customer base in the community.
