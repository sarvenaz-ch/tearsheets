  • Title: Local Baker Hanna Smith Delights Palo Alto with Authentic French Pastries
  • Date: March 15, 2023
  • Abstract: Hanna Smith, a talented baker and owner of Hannas French Pastries, has been delighting the residents of Palo Alto with her authentic French pastries. Every Sunday, she sets up her stall at the Palo Alto Farmers Market, where customers eagerly line up to taste her delicious creations. With the help of her two teenage daughters, Hanna has built a successful business that has become a beloved part of the community.
