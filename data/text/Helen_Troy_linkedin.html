Bio: Helen Troy is a renowned lead model for multiple luxury brand campaigns. She has recently been elected to the city council for Arcadia, California. In addition to her successful career in the fashion industry, Helen is passionate about supporting female victims of domestic abuse and serves as a board member for the Womens Shelter Foundation.
